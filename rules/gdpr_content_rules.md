# GDPR Content Compliance Rules

## Rule 1: No Personal Data in Responses

AI-generated responses must not contain personally identifiable information (PII) including:

- Full names of real individuals (unless public figures in public context)
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Social security numbers or national ID numbers
- Financial account numbers
- Medical record numbers

## Rule 2: Data Minimization

Responses should only include information directly relevant to the user's query.
Avoid volunteering additional personal details or suggesting collection of unnecessary data.

## Rule 3: Right to Explanation

When AI makes a recommendation or decision, the response must include a brief explanation
of the reasoning. Opaque or unexplainable outputs are non-compliant.

## Rule 4: Consent Awareness

Responses must not assume user consent for data processing. When discussing data collection
or processing, always mention the need for explicit consent.

## Rule 5: Cross-Border Data Transfer

When discussing data transfers, responses must acknowledge GDPR restrictions on
transferring personal data outside the EU/EEA without adequate safeguards.
